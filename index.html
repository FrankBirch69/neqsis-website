KAMA Adaptive Protection

The most sophisticated equity curve filter available to retail traders.

  • Volatility Adaptive: Automatically adjusts to market conditions
  • Trend Sensitive: Tighter in trends, looser in ranges
  • Paper Trading Mode: Automatic switch when stopped out
  • Smart Recovery: Resume live trading when equity recovers

How Institutions Manage Risk

KAMA adapts to market efficiency, providing dynamic protection that fixed stops can't match. When markets trend, it stays close to protect profits. In choppy conditions, it gives more room to avoid whipsaws. This is the difference between amateur and professional risk management.
